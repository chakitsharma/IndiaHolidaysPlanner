Why To Visit Rajasthan

  • Rajasthan is home to the Amber Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of India's most magnificent fortresses.
  • The Thar Desert in Jaisalmer offers unique experiences like camel safaris, desert camping, and folk music.
  • Udaipur, known as the "City of Lakes," is one of the most romantic and picturesque cities in India.
  • Rajasthan boasts over 30,000 temples, hundreds of forts, and palaces showcasing its royal heritage.
  • The Ranthambore National Park is one of the best places in India to spot the Royal Bengal Tiger.
  • Pushkar is home to the only Brahma Temple in the world and hosts the famous Pushkar Camel Fair.
  • Rajasthan's Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds) in Jaipur has 953 windows and is an architectural wonder.
