Privacy Policy for India Holiday Planner

Effective Date: March 28, 2026
Last Updated: March 28, 2026

At India Holiday Planner, accessible from https://indiaholidaysplanner.com/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by India Holiday Planner and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our domestic tour packages.

  • Identity Data: Name.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Booking Details: Number of persons traveling, preferred dates, and destination interests.
  • Payment Information: For online payments, credit card details are collected by our third-party payment gateway; we do not store this sensitive data on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our travel services.
  • To process your bookings and communicate travel itineraries.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our website experience.
  • To send you promotional emails or newsletters (only with your explicit opt-in consent).
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our platform.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Hotels, transport providers, and guides necessary to fulfill your booked tour.
  • Legal Reasons: If required by Indian law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Security and Retention

We use commercially acceptable security measures, such as SSL encryption, to protect your personal data. We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent for marketing at any time.

6. Children's Information

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 18 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
